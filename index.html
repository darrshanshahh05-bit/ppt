Who We Are

A Bridge Between Abundance & Need

Prabhu Premi Trust (PPT) is a faith-inspired NGO rooted in Jain values. For us, PPT holds a far deeper, more profound meaning: Prabhu (The Divine), Premi (One who loves with devotion), and Trust (A solemn responsibility to serve).

"Only a true Prabhu Premi — one who loves the Divine with a pure heart — feels the inner call to give, uplift, and serve without expectation."
