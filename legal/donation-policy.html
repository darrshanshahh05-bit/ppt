Donation Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At PrabhuPremi Trust, we are committed to complete transparency and accountability in handling your generous contributions. This policy explains how donations are processed, allocated, and utilized.

Donation Categories

When you donate, you may choose to support one of our seven pillars of service:

  1. Vaiyavachh — Service to Saints and Sangh
  2. Vihar Dham — Support for Jain monk travels
  3. Shiksha Seva — Education support programs
  4. Arogya Seva — Medical aid and health camps
  5. Sarva Sadharan — General welfare (default allocation)
  6. Jeevdaya — Animal welfare and protection
  7. Anukampadaan — Support for families in crisis

Note: If no specific category is selected, your donation will be directed to Sarva Sadharan (General Welfare), ensuring it is used where it's genuinely needed most.

How Funds Are Used

We are committed to maximizing the impact of every rupee donated:

  • Minimum 85% of donations go directly to program activities
  • Maximum 15% is used for administrative and operational costs
  • Designated donations are used exclusively for the selected purpose
  • Surplus funds may be carried forward to the next financial year

Digital Transparency

All donations are processed through secure digital platforms, enabling:

  • Instant donation receipts via email
  • Digital tracking of your contribution
  • Secure payment processing
  • Clear documentation for tax purposes

Tax Benefits

Donations to PrabhuPremi Trust may be eligible for tax exemptions under applicable sections of the Income Tax Act. Please provide your PAN details when donating to receive a tax-compliant receipt.

Note: Tax benefits are subject to prevailing laws. Please consult your tax advisor for specific advice.

Refund Policy

Donations are generally non-refundable as they represent charitable contributions. However, we may consider refund requests in the following cases:

  • Duplicate or erroneous transactions
  • Technical errors during payment processing
  • Significant deviation from the intended donation amount

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the donation. Contact us at info@prabhupremitrust.org with your transaction details.

Recurring Donations

If you set up a recurring donation, you may cancel at any time by contacting us. Cancellation will take effect from the next billing cycle. No refunds will be issued for donations already processed.

Privacy of Donors

We respect your privacy. Donor information is kept confidential and is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Anonymous Donations

We welcome anonymous donations. However, please note that anonymous donors may not be eligible for tax benefits as we cannot issue receipts without identification details.

Reporting & Accountability

We provide regular updates on how donations are utilized through:

  • Annual reports available on request
  • Impact stories shared via newsletter
  • Financial audits conducted annually
  • Regular updates on our website and social media
